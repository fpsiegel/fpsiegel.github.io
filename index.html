What is the Seppie Lab ?

The Seppie Lab is a 48 m² space with ground floor access and shop front located in 17, Via Genova, Amantea, Calabria, IT.

    It is equipped with:
  • 1-3 operators -particpiating on their own initiatives
  • 2 sewing machines -provided by Carhartt WiP
  • 2 computers -provided by different sources
  • 5 tables, 7 chairs, 3 benches -provided by different sources
  • 1 high speed internet connection -provided by Le Seppie
    It is NOT equipped with:
  • Enough computers
  • A heater
  • A fully functionning door
  • An access to water
  • An access to toilets
