Part I - What is a computer ?

The first task will be for you to research on the internet about the different components of a computer , by using a search engine such as: google, bing or duckduckgo. Once you are able to demonstrate a good understanding of those, move on to researching about the input and ouput devices that allow us to use the computer. Finally, conclude with a small research about the internet
